# National Chung Hsing University Guidelines for the Network Management of Student Dormitories

## Article 1

### Keywords

purpose, campus network, resource management

### Chinese Text

為充分發揮校園網路功能、落實網路資源合理使用與有效管理學生宿舍網路，特訂定本要點。

### English Translation

The Guidelines are formulated to give full play to the functions of the campus network, implement the rational use of network resources, and effectively manage the network of student dormitories.

### Structured Summary

- Purpose: maximize campus network functions, ensure rational use, and effectively manage dormitory network

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 2

### Keywords

compliance, campus network regulations, IP address

### Chinese Text

凡住宿學生使用宿舍網路，須依「國立中興大學校園網路使用規範」使用，及本校計算機及資訊網路中心所公佈的IP 網路位址，使用網路資源。

### English Translation

All boarding students who use the dormitory network shall conform to the "Campus Network Usage Regulations of National Chung Hsing University" and the IP network addresses designated by the Computer and Information Network Center to use network resources.

### Structured Summary

- Dormitory network users must follow Campus Network Usage Regulations and use designated IP addresses

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 3

### Keywords

prohibited acts, suspension, threatening, virus, spam, hacking, porn

### Chinese Text

如有下列情事發生，並經查獲屬實，除依相關法規處分外，將予以停權處分。
(一) 傳送威脅性、猥褻性、商業性或不友善的資料。
(二) 散佈病毒，或侵入未經授權使用的電腦系統。
(三) 大量傳送信件，或對不同主機大量掃描。
(四) 使用或傳送未經授權之任何檔案。
(五) 蓄意破壞或不正當使用網路設備。
(六) 架設非法或色情之不良網站。
(七) 使用他人之 IP 網路位址者。
待上述停權原因終止後，得申請恢復使用權。

### English Translation

If any of the following occurs and is found to be true, students will be deprived of the right to use the Internet in addition to being punished in accordance with relevant laws and regulations.
(1) Transmit threatening, obscene, commercial, or unfriendly information.
(2) Spread viruses, or invade unauthorized computer systems.
(3) Send a large number of letters, or scan numerous hosts.
(4) Use or transmit any files without authorization.
(5) Intentional damage or improper use of network devices.
(6) Set up illegal or pornographic websites.
(7) Use the IP network address of others.
An application may be made for the restoration of the right of use after the reasons above for suspension terminate.

### Structured Summary

- Prohibited acts: (1) threatening/obscene/commercial/unfriendly content, (2) viruses/unauthorized access, (3) mass emails/scanning, (4) unauthorized files, (5) intentional damage/misuse of equipment, (6) illegal/pornographic websites, (7) using others' IP addresses
- Penalty: network suspension + relevant legal punishment
- May apply for reinstatement after cause resolved

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 4

### Keywords

traffic control, bandwidth, data limit, Taiwan Academic Network

### Chinese Text

為保持網路暢通，依「臺灣學術網路管理規範」，對網路流量須做適當之區隔、管控。單日上傳或下載流量依中部區網中心規定，並以本校計算機及資訊網路中心公告限額為管制執行依據(校內互傳不列入計算)，使用量超出管制額度之IP，自超出規定額度之時間起，依本校網路流量管制機制，限制網路頻寬使用。

### English Translation

To keep the network smooth, the data flow shall be properly separated and controlled according to the "Taiwan Academic Network Management and Norms". The daily upload or download of data flow shall be subject to the regulations of the Central District Network Center and shall be controlled based on the limit announced by the Computer and Information Network Center of NCHU (intra-campus mutual transmission shall not be included in the calculation). The use of network bandwidth will be restricted in accordance with the NCHU's data flow control mechanism from the time when the limit is exceeded.

### Structured Summary

- Traffic managed per Taiwan Academic Network Management and Norms
- Daily upload/download limits set by Central District Network Center and NCHU Computer Center
- Intra-campus traffic excluded from limit calculation
- Bandwidth restricted when limit exceeded

### Penalty Points

None specified

If the dormitory network is faulty, please apply to the network administrator for repair.

### Structured Summary

- Network faults: report to network administrator for repair

### Penalty Points

None specified

supervision, Computer and Information Network Center

### English Translation

The management and operation of the dormitory network shall be supervised by the Computer and Information Network Center of NCHU.

### Structured Summary

- Dormitory network management and operation supervised by Computer and Information Network Center

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 7

### Keywords

supplementary, MOE regulations, Taiwan Academic Network

### Chinese Text

本要點如果有未盡事宜，悉依「教育部校園網路使用規範」、「臺灣學術網路管理規範」及本校相關法規辦理。

### English Translation

If there are any matters not covered in the Guidelines, please follow the "Ministry of Education Regulations for the Access to Campus Network", "Taiwan Academic Network Management and Norms", and relevant regulations of NCHU.

### Structured Summary

- Matters not covered: follow MOE Campus Network Regulations, Taiwan Academic Network Management and Norms, and NCHU regulations

### Penalty Points

None specified

After the Guidelines are approved by the student affairs meeting, they will be presented to the principal for approval and implementation, and the same applies to amendments.

### Structured Summary
