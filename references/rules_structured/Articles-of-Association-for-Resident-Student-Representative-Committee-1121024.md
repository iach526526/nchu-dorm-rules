# National Chung Hsing University Articles of Association for Resident Student Representative Committee

## Article 1

### Keywords

purpose, self-government, participation, representation

### Chinese Text

為培養自治精神、增進住宿生參與宿舍管理、蒐集住宿生意見及改善住宿環境，依據「國立中興大學學生住宿輔導辦法」設立住宿生代表會（以下簡稱「本會」）。

### English Translation

In order to foster the spirit of self-government, enhance the participation of residents in dormitory management, collect advise from residents, and improve the living environment, a Resident Student Representative Committee (hereinafter referred to as "the Committee") is established according to Dormitory Regulations of National Chung Hsing University.

### Structured Summary

- Purpose: foster self-government, increase participation, collect opinions, improve living environment
- Established per NCHU Dormitory Regulations

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 2

### Keywords

supervision, OSA, Student Housing Service Division

### Chinese Text

本會由學務處住宿輔導組成立，接受住宿輔導組之指導及監督。

### English Translation

The Committee is established by Division of Student Housing Service in Office of Student Affairs and is subject to guidance and supervision of Division of Student Housing Service.

### Structured Summary

- Established by OSA Student Housing Service Division; guided and supervised by them

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 3

### Keywords

legal basis, dormitory regulations

### Chinese Text

本章程根據本校學生住宿輔導辦法相關規定制定之。

### English Translation

The articles of association are formulated according to the relevant regulations in Dormitory Regulations of National Chung Hsing University.

### Structured Summary

- Based on NCHU Dormitory Regulations

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 4

### Keywords

structure, chairman, representatives

### Chinese Text

本會設置主席一名及住宿生代表等若干名。

### English Translation

The Committee has a chairman and several representatives of resident students.

### Structured Summary

- Structure: 1 chair + several representatives

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 5

### Keywords

responsibilities, suggestions, regulation amendments

### Chinese Text

本會之職掌如下：
一、彙集住宿同學之建議，轉知住宿輔導組報請學校有關單位
二、協助宿舍管理之相關規章之修訂。

### English Translation

The responsibilities of the Committee are as follows:
1. Collect advises from resident students and notify division of student housing service of NCHU to report to relevant divisions.
2. Assist in the amendment of dormitory relevant regulations.

### Structured Summary

- Collect resident suggestions and forward to Housing Service Division
- Assist in amending dormitory regulations

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 6

### Keywords

duties, chairman, representative

### Chinese Text

本會幹部之職掌：
一、主席
(一)每學期召開至少一次住宿生代表會會議。
(二)對外代表本會，對內管理本會業務。
二、住宿生代表：
(一)負責蒐集住宿生意見。
(二)提供住宿生活興革事項。
(三)協助宿舍相關規章修訂。

### English Translation

Responsibilities of the cadres of the Committee:
1. Chairman
(1) Hold at least one resident student representative meeting for each semester.
(2) Represent the Committee externally and manage the business of the Committee internally.
2. Representative of resident students
(1) Collect advises from resident students.
(2) Providing suggestions related to dormitory life and environments.
(3) Assist in the amendment of dormitory relevant regulations.

### Structured Summary

- Chair: hold ≥1 meeting/semester; represent committee; manage internal affairs
- Representatives: collect opinions; provide improvement suggestions; assist regulation amendments

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 7

### Keywords

meeting, quorum, resolution

### Chinese Text

本會會議以每學期至少召開一次為原則，由主席負責主持會議，得邀請學務處相關長官、住宿輔導組組長及宿舍服務中心代表出席指導。議決事項時應有二分之一以上代表出席，且三分之二以上同意始得決議，決議事項送住宿輔導組備查。

### English Translation

The Committee shall hold at least one meeting for each semester. The chairman is responsible for presiding over the meeting. The director of the Office of Student Affairs, the head of the Division of Student Housing Service, and the representative of the Dormitory Service Center may be invited to attend the meeting. A resolution shall be made only when more than half of the representatives are present, and more than two-thirds of them agree with it. The resolution shall be reported to the Division of Student Housing Service.

### Structured Summary

- At least 1 meeting/semester; chair presides
- OSA director, Housing Service head, Service Center representative may attend
- Quorum: >50%; resolution: >2/3 approval; reported to Housing Service Division

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 8

### Keywords

interim meeting, petition

### Chinese Text

本會得經二分之一以上代表連署召開臨時會議，主席應於十日內召開之。

### English Translation

The Committee may convene a temporary meeting with the joint signature of more than half of the representatives, and the chairman shall call it within ten days.

### Structured Summary

- Interim meeting: >50% representatives petition; chair convenes within 10 days

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 9

### Keywords

attendance, obligation, leave

### Chinese Text

住宿生代表有參加住宿生代表會各項會議之義務，若因故未能參加會議，應向主席請假。

### English Translation

The resident student representatives are obliged to participate in various meetings of the Resident Student Representative Committee. If they fail to attend the meeting for any reason, they shall report to the chairperson.

### Structured Summary

- Representatives must attend meetings; if absent, must report to chair

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 10

### Keywords

amendment, supplementary, approval

### Chinese Text

本章程如有未盡事宜，由本住宿生代表會議擬訂，陳請學務長核定後公布實施，修正時亦同。

### English Translation

Any matters not covered in the Articles of Association may be revised by Resident Student Representative Committee and be approved by the Director of Office of Student Affairs before taking effect. Amendments shall be processed accordingly.

### Structured Summary

- Matters not covered: revised by Resident Student Representative Committee; approved by Director of OSA

### Penalty Points

None specified
