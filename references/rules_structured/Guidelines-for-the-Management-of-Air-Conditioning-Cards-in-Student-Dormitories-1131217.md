# Guidelines for the Management of Air Conditioning Cards in Student Dormitories at National Chung Hsing University

## Section 1: Purpose

### Keywords

purpose, air conditioning card, management, recycling, reward

### Chinese Text

為妥善管理學生宿舍冷氣卡之使用及環保回收獎勵機制，訂定本要點。

### English Translation

These guidelines are established to ensure the efficient management of air conditioning card usage in student dormitories and to implement an environmentally friendly recycling reward mechanism.

### Structured Summary

- Purpose: manage AC card usage and implement eco-friendly recycling reward system

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Section 2: Scope of Application

### Keywords

scope, male dormitories, female dormitories, public areas

### Chinese Text

(一)男宿：仁齋、義齋、禮齋、智齋、信齋。
(二)女宿：勤軒、怡軒、華軒。
(三)宿舍公共區域：例如公共廚房等。

### English Translation

1. Male Dormitories: Renzhai Building, Yizhai Building, Lizhai Building, Zhizhai Building, and Xinzhai Building.
2. Female Dormitories: Qinxuan Building, Yixuan Building, Huaxuan Building.
3. Public Dormitory Areas: Including communal facilities such as shared kitchens.

### Structured Summary

- Male dorms: Renzhai, Yizhai, Lizhai, Zhizhai, Xinzhai
- Female dorms: Qinxuan, Yixuan, Huaxuan
- Public areas: shared kitchens, etc.

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Section 3: Fees and Recharge Mechanism

### Keywords

user pays, chip card, purchase, recharge, fee

### Chinese Text

(一)學生宿舍冷氣費用依使用者付費原則，以採晶片儲值卡(卡片為損耗品，採買斷制)插卡使用，欲使用宿舍冷氣，以寢室為單位，每度電收費依學校公告費率計價。
(二)新卡購買：於宿舍服務中心之售卡機購買冷氣晶片儲值卡(全新)，應繳納卡片費一百元(製卡成本五十元，卡內儲值五十元)，每張卡片儲值額度使用完畢時可於加值機再重複儲值。
(三)舊卡購買：於住宿輔導組購買二手舊卡，每張空卡售價二十元。
(四)儲值金額如有疑義，由宿舍服務中心協助處理。

### English Translation

1. Air conditioning fees in student dormitories operate on a "user pays" principle. Students must use a chip-based rechargeable card to activate the air conditioning system. (The cards are consumable items sold outright.) Electricity charges are calculated per room based on the rate announced by the Housing Assistance Office.
2. New air conditioning chip cards can be purchased from vending machines at the Dormitory Service Center. Each card costs NT$100, which includes a NT$50 production fee and an initial stored value of NT$50. Once the balance is depleted, the card can be recharged using an add-value machine.
3. Second-hand air conditioning cards can be purchased from the Housing Assistance Office for NT$20 per card.
4. Students with questions about their card balance may seek assistance from the Dormitory Service Center.

### Structured Summary

- User-pays principle; chip card required; per-room charging at university rate
- New card: NT$100 (NT$50 production + NT$50 stored value) from vending machines; rechargeable
- Second-hand card: NT$20 from Housing Assistance Office
- Balance inquiries: contact Service Center

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Section 4: Environmental Recycling Mechanism

### Keywords

recycling, balance refund, EasyCard, environmental reward

### Chinese Text

(一)離宿時可先將冷氣晶片儲值卡至多功能繳費機辦理退回儲值餘額至個人悠遊卡，再至住宿輔導組辦公室繳回已無餘額之冷氣晶片儲值卡。
(二)舊卡經測試卡片可正常使用後扣除折舊費，給予環保獎勵費二十元。

### English Translation

1. Upon checking out of the dormitory, students may use a multifunction payment machine to transfer any remaining balance from their air conditioning card to their personal EasyCard. Afterward, the emptied air conditioning card must be returned to the Housing Assistance Office.
2. Used cards that pass functionality testing after accounting for depreciation will qualify for an environmental reward of NT$20.

### Structured Summary

- Check-out: transfer remaining balance to EasyCard via multifunction machine; return emptied card to Housing Assistance Office
- Used cards that pass testing: NT$20 environmental reward

### Penalty Points

None specified

approval, President, amendment

### English Translation

These guidelines shall come into effect upon approval by the President. Any amendments to the guidelines will follow the same approval procedure.

### Structured Summary

- Approved by President; same for amendments
