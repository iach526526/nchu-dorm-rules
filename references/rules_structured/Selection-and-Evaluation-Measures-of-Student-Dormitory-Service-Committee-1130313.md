# National Chung Hsing University Selection and Evaluation Measures of Student Dormitory Service Committee

## Article 1

### Keywords

legal basis, Dormitory Regulations

### Chinese Text

本辦法依據「國立中興大學學生住宿輔導辦法」訂定之。

### English Translation

The Measures are formulated in accordance with the "Dormitory Regulations of National Chung Hsing University".

### Structured Summary

- Based on NCHU Dormitory Regulations

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 2

### Keywords

selection, chairman, deputy, finance, network, service member, qualifications, criteria

### Chinese Text

本會委員之產生：
符合資格且具意願之同學，檢附有利遴選之相關證明文件，逕自向宿舍服務中心報名，由住宿輔導組、宿舍服務中心及學生代表共同組成遴選委員會遴選之。
一、主任委員、副主任委員
由遴選委員會依遴選評分成績擇優選派。
二、財務委員
由住宿輔導組及宿舍服務中心徵選具有記帳經歷或會計背景之住宿生。
三、網路管理委員
接受計算機中心之技術指導，經考試通過由住宿輔導組任命之。
四、服務委員
(一)遴選資格：
1. 當學年度全體住宿生，包含已錄取本校研究所之畢業生。（欲休學、轉學者請勿報名遴選）
2. 住宿一年以上，對宿舍管理事務具相當體認。
3. 上一學期學科總成績平均不得低於 60 分，操行成績不得低於 80 分。
4. 任期內未擔任學校社團及學生組織重要幹部者。
5. 住宿期間曾被勒令退宿、違規記點記錄三次（或逾 15 點）以上或曾自願放棄住宿者，不得報名遴選。
(二)任期為一學年（當年 7 月 1 日至次年 6 月 30 日），連選得連任，並必須參加管理委員交接暨研習活動。
(三)遴選審查標準：
1. （30%）面試
2. （15%）學科成績
3. （10%）品德操守
4. （20%）家庭經濟情況
5. （15%）宿舍服務與修繕技能
6. （10%）曾任學校幹部（含高中職期間或校外社團）之經歷與成效
(四)遴選人數不足實際需求員額，得於兩周內再次公告遴選，若仍不足，則由遴選委員會推薦任命之。

### English Translation

Selection of committee members:
Students who are qualified and willing to be committee members shall take relevant supporting documents favorable for selection to register with the Dormitory Service Center. A selection committee consisting of members from the Division of Student Housing Service, Dormitory Service Center, and student representatives will make the selection.
1. Chairman, Deputy Chairman
The Selection Committee will select the best candidates based on the selection scores.
2. Finance Member
Boarding students with bookkeeping experience or accounting background will be selected as finance members by the Division of Student Housing Service and the Dormitory Service Center.
3. Network Administrator
After receiving technical guidance from the Computer Center and passing the examination, the network administrator will be appointed by the Division of Student Housing Service.
4. Service Member
(1) Selection qualifications:
1). All boarding students in the current academic year, including graduates admitted to NCHU. (Please do not apply for the post if you want to take a break from school or transfer to another school)
2). Have stayed at least one year, and have good knowledge of dormitory management.
3). The average score of subjects in the last semester shall not be lower than 60, and the score in conduct shall not be lower than 80.
4). Those who have not served as an important cadre of school associations or student organizations during their term of office.
5). Those who have been ordered to leave the dorm during their stay, have three or more demerits (or more than 15 points), or have voluntarily given up their beds shall not apply for the selection.
(2) The tenure is one academic year (July 1st of the current year to June 30th of the following year). Those who are re-elected shall participate in the handover and study of the Management Committee.
(3) Selection review criteria:
1). (30%) interview
2). (15%) GPA
3). (10%) moral conduct
4). (20%) family financial situation
5). (15%) dormitory services and repair skills
6). (10%) experience and achievements of working as a school cadre (including senior high school, vocational school, or off-campus clubs).
(4) If the number of candidates is less than the number needed, the selection may be announced again within two weeks. If it does not work, the Selection Committee will make the appointment.

### Structured Summary

- Registration at Service Center with supporting documents; Selection Committee (Housing Service, Service Center, student representatives) decides
- Chair/Vice Chair: selected by score ranking
- Finance member: recruited from students with accounting/bookkeeping experience
- Network administrator: pass exam after Computer Center training; appointed by Housing Service
- Service member qualifications: current resident (including admitted grads); ≥1 year residence; GPA ≥60, conduct ≥80; no parallel important student org roles; no prior eviction/≥3 violations (or >15 points)/voluntary withdrawal
- Tenure: 1 academic year (July 1 – June 30); re-election allowed; must attend handover/training
- Selection criteria: interview 30%, GPA 15%, conduct 10%, family finances 20%, service/repair skills 15%, cadre experience 10%
- If insufficient applicants: re-announce within 2 weeks; if still insufficient, Selection Committee appoints

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 3

### Keywords

rights, bed guarantee, fee refund, reward, service certificate

### Chinese Text

學生宿舍服務委員權益：
一、保障任期內學生宿舍床位。
二、任期內考核成績達 70 分以上者將退住宿費、頒發學生宿舍優秀服務委員獎勵金（未獲得校內住宿補貼者，不頒發獎勵金）。
三、考核及格者頒發服務證明、簽請記功嘉獎。

### English Translation

Rights and interests of members of Student Dormitory Service Committee:
1. Obtain the dorm beds during the tenure.
2. Those who achieve a performance evaluation score of 70 or above will receive a refund of dormitory fees and be awarded the Outstanding Committee Members Monetary Award. (those who do not receive on-campus housing subsidies will not receive the monetary award).
3. Those who pass the assessment will receive service certificates and merit awards.

### Structured Summary

- Guaranteed dorm bed during tenure
- Assessment score ≥70: dorm fee refund + Outstanding Committee award (no award if no housing subsidy)
- Pass assessment: service certificate + merit recognition

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 4

### Keywords

dismissal, grounds, absence, grades, misconduct

### Chinese Text

學生宿舍服務委員任職期間之解職
服務委員若有下列情形之一者，得由原遴選委員會予以解職：
一、職掌範圍內應做而未做之事，住宿輔導組與宿舍服務中心得於查證屬實後，以書面通知該委員，經告知仍未改進者。
二、開會無故缺席兩次以上者。
三、任期內，二分之一學科不及格或辦理休學。
四、學期末考核成績未達 70 分者。
五、行為不當，足以破壞本會形象及名譽者。
六、違反本會組織章程。
七、職區內之全體住宿同學連署簽名達一半以上，並提出具體事實要求解職者。

### English Translation

Committee members may be dismissed by the original Selection Committee under any of the following circumstances:
1. For things that should be done but not done within the scope of their duties, the Division of Student Housing Service and the Dormitory Service Center may notify the members in writing after verifying the facts.
2. Absent from the meeting more than twice without a good reason.
3. During the tenure, those who fail one-half of their courses or are on leave of absence.
4. Those who fail to score 70 points in the assessment at the end of the semester.
5. Improper conduct that damages the image and reputation of the Committee.
6. Violate the Articles of Association.
7. More than half of all the boarding students under the administration of committee members sign their names and present specific facts to request dismissal.

### Structured Summary

- Dismissal grounds: (1) duties not performed after written notice, (2) unexcused absence from meetings ≥2 times, (3) failing ≥1/2 courses or taking leave, (4) assessment score <70, (5) misconduct damaging Committee reputation, (6) violating Articles of Association, (7) ≥50% of floor residents sign for dismissal with specific facts

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 6

### Keywords

assessment, scoring, evaluation criteria, rewards, penalties

### Chinese Text

學生宿舍服務委員考核規定：
一、職務任期內，於每學期期末考前一周舉行考核成績評比。
二、考核總評比例：
考核者：住宿輔導組宿舍服務中心 60%；服務委員主副委評分 30%；服務委員互評 10%
三、受評者考核評比項目：
(一)住宿輔導組及宿舍服務中心：工作態度 25%、工作能力 25%、工作績效 25%、溝通協調 25%
(二)服務委員主副委評分：工作態度 25%、工作能力 25%、工作績效 25%、溝通協調 25%
(三)服務委員互評：工作態度 25%、工作能力 25%、工作績效 25%、溝通協調 25%
四、主任委員或副主任委員，住宿輔導組及宿舍服務中心應視其職務內容及成效予以考核總成績最多 10 分增減。
五、學生宿舍服務委員考核獎懲標準，請詳見【附表三】。

### English Translation

Assessment regulations of members of Student Dormitory Service Committee:
1. During the tenure, the assessment will be made one week before the final exam of each semester.
2. Proportion of evaluation scoring: Division of Student Housing Service/Dormitory Service Center: 60%; Chairman and deputy chairman: 30%; Mutual evaluation of committee members: 10%
3. Items evaluated by appraisers: attitude toward work 25%, working competence 25%, job performance 25%, communication and coordination 25%
4. The chairman or deputy chairman, the Division of Student Housing Service, and the Dormitory Service Center may increase or decrease the total score of the assessment by up to 10 points depending on the job content and achievements.
5. Please refer to [Annex 3] for the assessment criteria for rewards and punishments for student dormitory service committee members.

### Structured Summary

- Assessment held one week before final exams each semester
- Scoring: Housing Service/Service Center 60%, Chair/Vice Chair 30%, Peer review 10%
- Criteria: work attitude 25%, competence 25%, performance 25%, communication 25%
- Chair/Vice Chair: ±10 points adjustment possible
- Rewards/penalties per Annex 3 (score tiers: ≥90, 89-85, 84-80, 79-75, 74-70, ≤69 with corresponding rewards/demerits)
