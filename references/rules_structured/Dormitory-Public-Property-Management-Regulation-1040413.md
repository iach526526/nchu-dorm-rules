# National Chung Hsing University Dormitory Public Property Management Regulation

## Article 1

### Keywords

purpose, public property, allocation, management, inspection, repair

### Chinese Text

為統一規定學生宿舍公有財產物品之領取、分配、保管、使用及檢修，以達愛護公物，物盡其用之目的，特訂定本辦法。

### English Translation

This regulation is established to regulate the usage, allocation, management, inspection and repair of the public property of dormitory. Each student should maintain and keep the public property in good condition.

### Structured Summary

- Purpose: regulate receiving, allocation, storage, use, and repair of public property
- Goal: encourage care for public property and maximize utility

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 2

### Keywords

property manager, appointment, OSA supervision

### Chinese Text

男女生宿舍，設公有財產物品管理員一人（以下簡稱管理員），受學生事務處住宿輔導組督導。

### English Translation

Both boy and girl dormitory will be assigned a public property manager who is under the supervision of the Student Housing Services Division of the Office of Student Affairs.

### Structured Summary

- Each male/female dormitory has a property manager; supervised by OSA Student Housing Service Division

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 3

### Keywords

manager responsibilities, supervision, inspection, budget, maintenance

### Chinese Text

學生宿舍管理員，其職責如下：
一、督導與維護公共設施，財產之安全，並隨時檢查登記。
二、辦理財物申請、分配、送修、廢品繳交、損耗、報銷手續，並取據及登記。
三、編列年度修繕預算，並依先後順序，有效控制之。
四、配合有關單位對學生宿舍環境之整理。

### English Translation

The responsibility of student dormitory manager includes:
1. Supervise students and maintain the public property in the dormitory by inspecting facilities regularly to ensure the safety of students.
2. In charge of the application, allocation, maintenance and disposal of the public property in the dormitory.
3. Make and manage annual maintenance budget efficiently.
4. Maintain the environment and manage the public property of the dormitory.

### Structured Summary

- Manager duties: (1) supervise and maintain public property safety, inspect and record regularly; (2) handle property applications, allocation, repair, disposal, and paperwork; (3) prepare and manage annual maintenance budget; (4) coordinate with relevant units

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 4

### Keywords

borrowing, registration, responsibility, compensation

### Chinese Text

學生宿舍內公共使用之公物，概由管理員分配登記，並向管理員出據借用，一經借用後，即自負保管之責，其有耗損、遺失或修理者，由管理員申請補充或修理，可報銷者，由管理員報銷，須賠償者，由使用人照價賠償。

### English Translation

Public properties in the dormitory should be allocated by the dormitory manager. Students should register before borrowing and should keep properties in good condition. Managers should apply for supplements, repairs and disposal if the properties are lost or damaged. And students who lost and damage the properties should make compensations for the loss.

### Structured Summary

- Public property allocated by manager; students sign to borrow
- Borrower responsible for safekeeping
- Damage/loss: compensation at full cost by user

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 5

### Keywords

semester preparation, inspection, repair, service committee

### Chinese Text

每學期開學前，管理員應將各宿舍學生所需物品檢修、補充準備齊全，妥交各宿舍服務委員，分配各寢室使用。

### English Translation

Before every semester, dormitory managers should inspect, repair and make sure the usability of the public property in the dormitory and hand over his/her job to the dormitory service committee members.

### Structured Summary

- Before each semester: manager inspects, repairs, and prepares property; hands to service committee for room distribution

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 6

### Keywords

property card, signature, responsibility

### Chinese Text

學生進住宿舍後，均須簽章在財產卡自行負責保管使用，財產卡由宿舍服務中心統一印發。

### English Translation

Students should all sign on the public property management card and keep the card to themselves. Public property card will be distributed by the dormitory service center.

### Structured Summary

- Students sign property management card upon move-in; card distributed by Service Center

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 7

### Keywords

vacation check-out, return, compensation, deposit deduction

### Chinese Text

每屆寒暑假期，學生離校時，應將所借公物，按物品實點交管理員，其有遺失或故意損壞者，即應賠償，賠償價款由財產保證金中扣除。

### English Translation

Students should give back the public properties they borrowed to their dormitory manager before summer and winter vacation. Students who lost or damage the properties should make compensation, which should be deducted from the guarantee property fee.

### Structured Summary

- Before breaks: return borrowed property to manager
- Loss/damage: compensation deducted from property deposit

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 8

### Keywords

shared property, joint responsibility, compensation, natural disaster

### Chinese Text

各寢室內不屬於個人使用之公共設施，如門窗玻璃、電燈等由全室學生共同蓋章保管，如有損壞，由損壞人賠償，查不出損壞人者，由全室學生共同賠償，其因天災或人力不可抗拒之原因損壞者，由住宿輔導組證明報銷，並申請補充。

### English Translation

Doors, windows, lamps and other public properties should be in charge of all students in the dormitory. Students should sign on the management form to take on mutual responsibility for public property. Properties that damaged should be compensated by the students who damage it; if one cannot find who is responsible for it then all students in the dormitory should compensate for it together. Dormitory Service Commission will apply for the disposal and supplement of the properties damaged due to natural disasters or unpredictable circumstances.

### Structured Summary

- Shared facilities (doors, windows, lights): all roommates jointly responsible
- Damage: responsible student pays; if unknown, all roommates share cost
- Natural disasters/force majeure: reported by Housing Service Division for write-off and replacement

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 9

### Keywords

inventory, after-vacation, list, maintenance, disposal

### Chinese Text

寒暑假放假後，管理員應將學生宿舍內所有公物財產，作全盤之清查整理，開列清單，以便分別辦理申請補充修繕繳交報廢諸事宜。

### English Translation

After the summer and winter vacation, dormitory manager should inspect, count the number of all public property and make a list for future reference of maintenance and disposal.

### Structured Summary

- After breaks: manager conducts full inventory, makes list for supplement, repair, and disposal

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 10

### Keywords

staff responsibility, supervision, borrowing, return

### Chinese Text

負責管理宿舍學生之工作人員，須協助管理員督導學生愛惜公物，遵守規定，辦理公物之借用出據繳交報銷及賠償諸事宜。

### English Translation

Staff who is in charge of the management of student dormitory should help managers encourage students to follow dormitory regulations, manage the borrowing, returning, compensation for and disposal of the public property.

### Structured Summary

- Dormitory staff: assist manager in supervising students, managing borrowing/return/compensation/disposal

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 11

### Keywords

accident, manager handling, escalation

### Chinese Text

住宿學生，如因公物發生事故，應由管理員處理，管理員不能處理者，報由住宿輔導組處理之。

### English Translation

Manager should help students who have accidents due to the using of public property. Managers who are not able to handle the situation should report to the Dormitory Service Commission.

### Structured Summary

- Accidents involving public property: manager handles; if unable, escalate to Housing Service Division

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 12

### Keywords

janitor, assistant, manager direction, maintenance

### Chinese Text

各宿舍工友及事務助理員，須依管理員指揮調度，以從事公共設施之維護與公物之領取分發繳交搬運保管等工作。

### English Translation

Janitors and dormitory assistant should follow the order of the dormitory manager to allocate and maintain the public property of the dormitory.

### Structured Summary

- Janitors and assistants follow manager's direction for maintenance, distribution, and storage

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 13

### Keywords

care, damage, loss, compensation, demerit

### Chinese Text

凡住宿學生使用之公物必須妥善保管及愛護，不得有損壞或遺失者，應照原物賠償，如故意損壞者，簽予記大過以上之處分。

### English Translation

Students should maintain and keep the public property in good condition. Students who lost or damage the public property should compensate with the same property and students who deliberately damage the property will be given a demerit.

### Structured Summary

- Students must properly maintain public property
- Loss/damage: compensate with same item
- Intentional damage: demerit (major offense) or higher

### Penalty Points

- Intentional damage: demerit (major offense)

amendment, President, approval, implementation

本辦法陳請校長核定後公布實施，修正時亦同。

### English Translation

The regulations shall be implemented after being approved by the president. Any amendments shall be processed accordingly.

### Structured Summary

- Approved by President; same for amendments

### Penalty Points

None specified
