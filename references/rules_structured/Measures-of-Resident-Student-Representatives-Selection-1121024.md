# National Chung Hsing University Measures of Resident Student Representatives Selection

## Article 1

### Keywords

legal basis, Dormitory Regulations

### Chinese Text

本辦法依據「國立中興大學學生住宿輔導辦法」訂定之。

### English Translation

This measure is formulated according to Dormitory Regulations of National Chung Hsing University.

### Structured Summary

- Based on NCHU Dormitory Regulations

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 2

### Keywords

selection, chairman, representative, recommendation, quota

### Chinese Text

本會幹部之產生：
一、主席由住宿生代表互選產生。
二、住宿生代表應為當學年住宿生，且具服務熱忱者，於公開徵選時間內，獲得30位住宿生(該區住宿生不滿30人時，不在此限內)連署同意推薦產生。
三、代表人數每棟至少一人，按各棟住宿人數在三百人以下者，得設代表一人；住宿人數在三百(不含)至六百人以下者，得設置二人，六百人(不含)至九百人者得設置三人，九百人(不含)以上得設置四人。
四、當住宿生代表人數不足時，得由住宿輔導組推薦服務委員(排除主、副委)兼任。

### English Translation

Selection of the members for the committee:
1. Chairman: Selected from representatives of resident students.
2. Representatives shall be the resident of academic year and shall be with enthusiasm. Resident student representatives should be recommended by 30 residents during the public selection period (this limit does not apply if there are less than 30 residents in the area).
3. There must be at least one representative in each building. If the number of accommodations in each building is less than 300 people, one representative may be appointed; if the number of accommodations in each building is between 300 (exclusive) and 600 persons or less, there may be two representatives. If the number of accommodations in each building is from 600 to 900, there may be three representatives, and if there are more than 900 accommodations in each building, four representatives will be required.
4. When the number of representatives is insufficient, Members of Student Dormitory Service Committee recommend by the Division of Student Housing Service (the chairperson and vice chairperson excluded) may doubling as the resident student representatives.

### Structured Summary

- Chair: elected by representatives
- Representatives: current residents with enthusiasm; recommended by ≥30 residents (unless <30 total in area)
- Quota: at least 1/building; buildings <300 residents: 1; 301-600: 2; 601-900: 3; >900: 4
- Shortfall: Housing Service may recommend service committee members (excluding chair/vice chair) to double as representatives

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 3

### Keywords

tenure, academic year

### Chinese Text

住宿生代表任期為一學年。（開學第 3 週起至次年 6 月 30 日）

### English Translation

The term of resident student representatives is an academic year. (From the third week of the academic year to June 30 of the following year)

### Structured Summary

- Term: academic year (from week 3 to June 30)

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 4

### Keywords

rights, service certificate, assessment, bed guarantee

### Chinese Text

住宿生代表權益：
一、頒發服務證明。
二、任期期滿辦理獎懲，由住宿輔導組辦理考核，考核分數達80分(含)以上，保障下學年住宿床位。

### English Translation

Rights and interests of resident student representatives:
1. Obtain service certificates.
2. Receive rewards or punishments upon expiration of the term of office. Those who get a score of 80 in the assessment by Division of Student Housing Service will receive a guarantee of accommodation for next academic year.

### Structured Summary

- Service certificate upon completion
- Assessment by Housing Service; score ≥80: guaranteed bed for next academic year

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 5

### Keywords

dismissal, misconduct, absence, petition

### Chinese Text

住宿生代表若有下列情形之一者，得由其他住宿生代表決議後解職：
一、行為不當，經住宿輔導組認定，足以破壞本會形象及名譽者。
二、開會無故缺席一次以上者。
三、其他住宿生代表過半數要求更換者。
四、住宿生代表自行提出申請。

### English Translation

A representative may be dismissed according to resolutions by other representatives of each department when any of the following circumstances apply:
1. Improper conduct that damages the image and reputation of the committee.
2. Absence from the meeting more than one time without reason.
3. More than half of the representatives request a replacement.
4. Application by the representatives themselves.

### Structured Summary

- Dismissal grounds: (1) misconduct damaging reputation, (2) unexcused absence ≥1 meeting, (3) >50% representatives request replacement, (4) self-application

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 6

### Keywords

replacement, re-selection

### Chinese Text

住宿生代表之遞補，依本辦法重新產生之。

### English Translation

The replacement of resident student representatives shall be made according to this measure.

### Structured Summary

- Replacement: re-selection per these Measures

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 7

### Keywords

amendment, Resident Student Representative Committee, approval

### Chinese Text

本辦法住宿生代表會議通過擬訂，陳請學務長核定後公布實施，修正時亦同。

### English Translation

This measure is formulated by Resident Student Representative Committee and be approved by the Director of Office of Student Affairs before taking effect. Amendments shall be processed accordingly.

### Structured Summary

- Formulated by Resident Student Representative Committee; approved by Director of OSA

### Penalty Points

None specified
