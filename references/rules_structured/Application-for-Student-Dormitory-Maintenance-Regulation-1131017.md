# National Chung Hsing University Application for Student Dormitory Maintenance Regulation

## Article 1

### Keywords

purpose, repair, maintenance, facility

### Chinese Text

為維護學生宿舍生活設施之完善，加強修繕速度及修繕品質，特訂本辦法。

### English Translation

This regulation is established to enhance the speed and the quality of dormitory repair and to maintain the facility in the dormitory.

### Structured Summary

- Purpose: improve repair speed and quality; maintain dormitory facilities

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 2

### Keywords

adjustment, situation, university administrative system

### Chinese Text

依據實際狀況需要及本校行政體系訂定之。

### English Translation

This regulation can be adjusted according to different situations and the demands of the University.

### Structured Summary

- Regulation adjustable according to actual needs and university administration

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 3

### Keywords

maintenance responsibility, budget threshold, OGA, OSA

### Chinese Text

總務處營繕組負責金額壹拾伍萬元以上之修繕事宜；學生事務處住宿輔導組負責修繕申請、彙整、協調聯繫及金額壹拾伍萬元以下之修繕事宜。

### English Translation

Maintenance above NT$150,000 dollars will be handled by the Construction and Maintenance Section of the Office of General Affairs; application, management and contact of the maintenance under NT$150,000 dollars will be handled by the Students Housing Service Section of the Office of Students Affairs.

### Structured Summary

- Above NT$150,000: handled by OGA Construction and Maintenance Section
- Below NT$150,000: handled by OSA Student Housing Service Section

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 4

### Keywords

maintenance application, plumbing, construction, online reporting

### Chinese Text

修繕申請：
一、水電部份（包括公共區域電燈、抽水馬桶、水管漏水、飲水機、冷熱水、鍋爐、脫水機、車棚燈及寢室內插座、燈具、電話等）。
二、土木部份（包括公共區域牆、地板、門窗、水溝及寢室內床、櫃子、桌子、門窗等）。
如需即時處理者，請向宿舍服務中心或宿舍值班人員報告；如為一般損壞請至學務處資訊系統學生宿舍線上報修，以便安排修繕。

### English Translation

Maintenance application:
1. Plumbing, water and electricity supply (such as lights in public area, toilet, leaky pipe, hot and cold water supply, water cooler, boiler, clothes dryer, lights in carport, wall sockets, lights and telephones in the dormitory room)
2. Construction (such as the wall, floor, doors, windows, drainage system in the public area and beds, cabinets, doors and windows in the dormitory room)
Students who need immediate help shall report to the dormitory service center or the staff on duty. For general damages, students shall go to the website of the Students Affairs Information System to send a online application for repair.

### Structured Summary

- Plumbing/electrical: lights, toilets, pipes, water, boilers, dryers, sockets, phones
- Construction: walls, floors, doors, windows, drains, beds, cabinets, desks
- Emergency: report to Service Center or duty staff immediately
- General damage: online report via OSA information system

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 5

### Keywords

follow-up, service center, repair tracking

### Chinese Text

線上填寫請修單後，三天未見修繕者，請於上班時間至服務中心查詢、登記，以便催修，否則視同已修復。（需辦請購手續者其修復時間不在此限）

### English Translation

Students who fill out online application form but do not receive help within three days should go to the service center during office hours to check and register. If not, the maintenance application will be regarded as completed. (Application for procurement of new facility is not limited by this rule)

### Structured Summary

- If no response within 3 days of online application: visit Service Center to follow up
- Otherwise treated as completed
- Procurement requests exempt from this timeline

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 6

### Keywords

public property, damage, compensation, maintenance

### Chinese Text

公共財產申請不易，請愛惜使用，若發現有使用不當、故意破壞或遺失情形，將依「學生宿舍公有財產物品保管辦法」要求照價賠償後再行修繕或補充。

### English Translation

Students should maintain and use the public properties in the dormitory appropriately. Students who deliberately damage or throw away public properties in the dormitory will be asked to pay the maintenance and procurement expenses according to the "Dormitory Public Property Management Regulation."

### Structured Summary

- Use public property carefully
- Misuse, deliberate damage, or loss: compensate per Public Property Management Regulation before repair/replacement

### Penalty Points

None specified

## Article 7

### Keywords

amendment, approval, President, implementation

### Chinese Text

本辦法如有未盡事宜，經權責單位修正後，送請校長核定後，公告實施。

### English Translation

The regulations shall be implemented after being approved by the president. Any amendments shall be processed accordingly.

### Structured Summary

- Matters not covered: amended by responsible unit, approved by President, announced

### Penalty Points

None specified
