# National Chung Hsing University Directives Governing the Use and Management of Student Dormitory Refrigerators

### Keywords

### English Translation

These Directives are formulated to promote great living for residents, and maintain hygiene and order for the use of public refrigerators.

- Purpose: promote good living, maintain refrigerator hygiene and order

## Article 3

### Keywords

usage rules, storage, label, prohibited items, cleaning, disinfection

### Chinese Text

公用冰箱使用規定：
（一）學生宿舍公用冰箱僅提供本校住宿生使用。
（二）學生宿舍公用冰箱於每學期第一週開放使用，並於當學期第九週清空消毒暫停使用及第十八週結束後清空消毒，於寒暑假期間暫停開放，進行設備保養。
（三）冰放物品僅限於食品及藥品，並務必確實填寫存放單(附表)黏貼於盒上，冰放物品只能存放五天，如需續冰者請於存放單上更新日期；如為塑膠袋裝及散狀物品，應自備保鮮盒物品放置於其中並確實密封完整。
（四）冰存物品(含容器)若有以下情況者，將視為廢棄物處理，不得異議：
1. 管制藥品、動植物標本、化學溶劑、酒類、廚餘等。
2. 未確實填寫存放單者。
3. 非密封之食品。
4. 味道過重食品（熟食）:如臭豆腐、榴槤…等，影響他人使用冰箱權益者。
5. 冰存物品外盒裝長寬高總和逾 55 公分者。
6. 未按照第三點第三項存放者。
（五）如個人因素造成他人存放物品污損者，須照價賠償他人；造成冰箱環境髒亂者，須清潔打掃冰箱乙次。
（六）偷竊他人冰放物品者，將依宿舍規定記點，情節重大者，勒令退宿。
（七）請愛惜宿舍公用冰箱，妥善利用空間並隨手關緊冰箱門，存放冰箱之物品應自負保管責任；若物品遭清理、或遇不可抗拒因素(如停電、冰箱損壞等)致物品損壞，宿舍不負賠償責任。
（八）學生宿舍公用冰箱屬宿舍公有財產，若有住宿生破壞冰箱經查證屬實者，予以勒令退宿且不退宿費，並照價賠償。

### English Translation

Regulations for the use of public refrigerators:
(I) The public refrigerators in student dormitories are only for the use of residents.
(II) The public refrigerators in student dormitories shall be available for use in the first week every semester; suspended for use for cleaning and disinfection in the ninth week; and closed for use for cleaning and disinfection at the end of the eighteenth week. The refrigerators are under maintenance during the summer and winter breaks and are not available for use.
(III) Only food, drinks and drugs are allowed in the refrigerators. A Fridge Label (see Appendix) must be pasted on the container for storage. Items can only be stored for five days. If an item must be stored for more than five days, the date on the Fridge Label must be updated. If the item is kept in a plastic bag or are scattered things, they must be kept in airtight container and sealed tightly.
(IV) In the event of the following situations, the items stored (including the container) shall be disposed of and the owner shall not object:
1. Controlled drugs, plant and insect specimens, chemical solvents, alcohol, kitchen waste, etc.
2. Food or drugs without a Fridge Label, or with an incomplete Fridge Label.
3. Improperly sealed items.
4. Food (cooked meal) and drinks with a strong smell, e.g., stinky tofu, durian, which affect other users' rights.
5. A container with a total sum of length, width, and height exceeding 55 cm.
6. Items which are not stored according to provisions of Article 3, Paragraph 3.
(V) If a resident contaminates others' items for any reason, he or she shall be liable for compensation according to the cost price; if a resident contaminates the refrigerator, he or she shall be liable to clean the refrigerator once.
(VI) Residents who steal others' items shall be imposed a demerit point(s) according to dormitory regulations. If circumstances of the theft are serious, the resident may be expelled from the dormitory.
(VII) Residents are encouraged to cherish and make good use of the public refrigerators, and close the refrigerator door after use. Residents are responsible for safeguarding their respective items. If residents' items are cleared away, or damaged due to force majeure (e.g., power failure, damaged refrigerator), the dormitory administration shall not be responsible for compensation.
(VIII) Refrigerators in student dormitories are campus property. If residents are found to have damaged a refrigerator, they may be expelled from the dormitory with no accommodation fee refunded. Furthermore, they are liable for compensating the dormitory according to the cost price of the refrigerator.

### Structured Summary

- Refrigerators for residents only
- Schedule: open week 1; clean/disinfect week 9; close end of week 18; closed during breaks
- Allowed items: food and medicine only; must use Fridge Label; max 5 days; update date for extension; use sealed containers
- Disposable items: controlled drugs, specimens, solvents, alcohol, kitchen waste; no label; unsealed; strong smell; oversized (>55cm sum); improper storage
- Contamination: compensate victim or clean refrigerator
- Theft: demerit points; serious = eviction
- Use at own risk; force majeure not compensated
- Damaging refrigerator: eviction + no refund + full compensation

### Penalty Points

- Theft: demerit points per dormitory regulations
- Damaging refrigerator: eviction
