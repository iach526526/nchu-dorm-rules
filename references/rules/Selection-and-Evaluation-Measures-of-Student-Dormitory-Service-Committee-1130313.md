第一條
本辦法依據「國立中興大學學生住宿輔導辦法」訂定之。
Article 1 The Measures are formulated in accordance with the "Dormitory Regulations of National
Chung Hsing University".
第二條

本會委員之產生：
符合資格且具意願之同學，檢附有利遴選之相關證明文件，逕自向宿舍服務中心報名，由住
宿輔導組、宿舍服務中心及學生代表共同組成遴選委員會遴選之。
一、主任委員、副主任委員
由遴選委員會依遴選評分成績擇優選派。
二、財務委員
由住宿輔導組及宿舍服務中心徵選具有記帳經歷或會計背景之住宿生。
三、網路管理委員
接受計算機中心之技術指導，經考試通過由住宿輔導組任命之。
四、服務委員
(一) 遴選資格：（報名表詳見【附表一】）
1. 當學年度全體住宿生，包含已錄取本校研究所之畢業生。（欲休學、轉學者
請勿報名遴選）
2. 住宿一年以上，對宿舍管理事務具相當體認。
3. 上一學期學科總成績平均不得低於 60 分，操行成績不得低於 80 分。
4. 任期內未擔任學校社團及學生組織重要幹部者。
5. 住宿期間曾被勒令退宿、違規記點記錄三次（或逾 15 點）以上或曾自願放棄
住宿者，不得報名遴選。
(二) 任期為一學年（當年 7 月 1 日至次年 6 月 30 日），連選得連任，並必須參加管理
委員交接暨研習活動。
(三) 遴選審查標準：（遴選評分表詳見【附表二】）
1. （30%）面試
2. （15%）學科成績
3. （10%）品德操守
4. （20%）家庭經濟情況
5. （15%）宿舍服務與修繕技能
6. （10%）曾任學校幹部（含高中職期間或校外社團）之經歷與成效
(四) 遴選人數不足實際需求員額，得於兩周內再次公告遴選，若仍不足，則由遴選委
員會推薦任命之。

Article 2 Selection of committee members:
1

Students who are qualified and willing to be committee members shall take relevant
supporting documents favorable for selection to register with the Dormitory Service Center.
A selection committee consisting of members from the Division of Student Housing Service,
Dormitory Service Center, and student representatives will make the selection.
1. Chairman, Deputy Chairman
The Selection Committee will select the best candidates based on the selection
scores.
2. Finance Member
Boarding students with bookkeeping experience or accounting background will be
selected as finance members by the Division of Student Housing Service and the
Dormitory Service Center.
3. Network Administrator
After receiving technical guidance from the Computer Center and passing the
examination, the network administrator will be appointed by the Division of Student
Housing Service.
4. Service Member
(1) Selection qualifications: (Please refer to the registration form in [Annex 1])
1). All boarding students in the current academic year, including graduates
admitted to NCHU. (Please do not apply for the post if you want to take a break
from school or transfer to another school)
2). Have stayed at least one year, and have good knowledge of dormitory
management.
3). The average score of subjects in the last semester shall not be lower than 60,
and the score in conduct shall not be lower than 80.
4). Those who have not served as an important cadre of school associations or
student organizations during their term of office.
5). Those who have been ordered to leave the dorm during their stay, have three
or more demerits (or more than 15 points), or have voluntarily given up their
beds shall not apply for the selection.
(2) The tenure is one academic year (July 1st of the current year to June 30th of the
following year). Those who are re-elected shall participate in the handover and
study of the Management Committee.
(3) Selection review criteria: (Please refer to the Selection Rating Sheet in [Annex
2])
1). (30%) interview
2). (15%) GPA
3). (10%) moral conduct
4). (20%) family financial situation
5). (15%) dormitory services and repair skills
6). (10%) experience and achievements of working as a school cadre (including
senior high school, vocational school, or off-campus clubs).
(4) If the number of candidates is less than the number needed, the selection may
be announced again within two weeks. If it does not work, the Selection
Committee will make the appointment.
第三條

學生宿舍服務委員權益：
一、保障任期內學生宿舍床位。
二、任期內考核成績達 70 分以上者將退住宿費、頒發學生宿舍優秀服務委員獎勵金（未獲得校內住
宿補貼者，不頒發獎勵金）。

三、考核及格者頒發服務證明、簽請記功嘉獎。
Article 3 Rights and interests of members of Student Dormitory Service Committee:
1. Obtain the dorm beds during the tenure.
2. Those who achieve a performance evaluation score of 70 or above will receive a refund
of dormitory fees and be awarded the Outstanding Committee Members Monetary
2

Award. (those who do not receive on-campus housing subsidies will not receive the
monetary award).
3. Those who pass the assessment will receive service certificates and merit awards.
第四條

學生宿舍服務委員任職期間之解職
服務委員若有下列情形之一者，得由原遴選委員會予以解職：
一、職掌範圍內應做而未做之事，住宿輔導組與宿舍服務中心得於查證屬實後，以書面通知
該委員，經告知仍未改進者。
二、開會無故缺席兩次以上者。
三、任期內，二分之一學科不及格或辦理休學。
四、學期末考核成績未達 70 分者。
五、行為不當，足以破壞本會形象及名譽者。
六、違反本會組織章程。
七、職區內之全體住宿同學連署簽名達一半以上，並提出具體事實要求解職者。

Article 4 Dismissal of committee members during the tenure
Committee members may be dismissed by the original Selection Committee under any of
the following circumstances:
1. For things that should be done but not done within the scope of their duties, the Division
of Student Housing Service and the Dormitory Service Center may notify the members in
writing after verifying the facts.
2. Absent from the meeting more than twice without a good reason.
3. During the tenure, those who fail one-half of their courses or are on leave of absence.
4. Those who fail to score 70 points in the assessment at the end of the semester.
5. Improper conduct that damages the image and reputation of the Committee.
6. Violate the Articles of Association.
7. More than half of all the boarding students under the administration of committee
members sign their names and present specific facts to request dismissal.
第五條
學生宿舍服務委員之遞補，依本辦法重新遴選之。
Article 5 The replacement of committee members shall be made in accordance with the Measures.
